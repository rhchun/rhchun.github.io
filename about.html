About Me

Hello, my name is Roy Chun. I am Korean-Canadian who studies computer science in University of Waterloo.

I grew up in Prince Edward Island and graduated from Charlottetown Rural High School.

I decided to become a programmer after watching the movie, 'The Social Network',

and I am working very hard to achieve my goal.

I am sufficiently knowledgeable with C, C++, Scheme, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My strongest trait as a person is my leadership. I have served as the team captain in my high school

varsity soccer team, and worked as a treasurer in the student government.

My hobbies include playing various sports, especially soccer, playing the guitar and piano,

and playing Hearthstone, an online card game developed by Blizzard Entertainment, Inc.
